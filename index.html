About The Event

Idea Ignite is a cutting-edge idea pitching competition designed to spotlight innovative concepts at the intersection of design, AI/ML, future mobility, and sustainability. We are looking for forward-thinkers who are passionate about harnessing technology and design to create impactful solutions for a sustainable world.

Why ?

  • Showcase Your Ideas :

    • Present your groundbreaking concepts to a panel of experts and industry leaders.

  • Networking Opportunities :

    • Connect with fellow innovators, potential collaborators, and influential figures in the fields of design, technology, and sustainability.

  • Prizes and Recognition :

    • Stand a chance to win exciting prizes and gain recognition for your innovative solutions.

    When and Where ?

    9:30 AM to 1:30 PM

    About Jyothi

  • Jyothi Engineering College (JEC), established in 2002 under the Catholic Archdiocese of Trichur, is a leading engineering institution in Kerala, affiliated with Kerala Technological University.
  • It offers BTech, MTech, and PhD programs and holds NAAC 'A' grade accreditation, with five undergraduate programs also accredited by the NBA.
  • JEC focuses on producing "T-Shaped" professionals with both deep expertise and broad 21st-century skills.
  • The college features an Integrated Industrial Incubation Centre (IIIC) with TATA Technologies and a Technology Business Incubator (TBI) to support innovation and entrepreneurship.

    • About AIDA

  • The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science launched its Bachelor of Technology program in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science in 2020, admitting 60 students in its inaugural year.
  • In 2021, the Artificial Intelligence and Data Science Association (AIDA) was established and has since organized numerous events.
  • AIDA has played a pivotal role in aligning both the department and its students with the latest technological advancements, encouraging experimentation across various domains.
  • The association is actively working on implementing new features and is on track to deliver a shippable product soon.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    1. What is the theme of the competition?

      2. The theme of the competition is sustainability. You are free to choose any domain—technology, healthcare, agriculture, energy, education, etc.—but your idea must contribute to creating a more sustainable future. This could involve environmental conservation, social equity, renewable resources, or any solution that promotes long-term sustainability.

    2. When and where is the event?

      3. The selected participants from round 1, will attend the event on 2nd October 2024, from 9:30 AM to 1:30 PM at Jyothi Engineering College, Thrissur.

    3. Who can participate in Idea Ignite?

      4. Idea Ignite is open to: Students, professionals, and innovators from all fields who are passionate about sustainability. Participation is by team only and individual participation is not encouraged No specific technical background is required—what matters most is your creativity and your commitment to finding sustainable solutions. If you have an idea that can contribute to a more sustainable future, we encourage you to participate!

    1. Will we get Participation Certificate?

      2. The participants who are selected for round 2, will get their Certificates.

    2. Can I update my idea after the initial submission?

      3. Once the registration deadline has passed, you won’t be able to modify your submission for Round 1 .However, if you’re shortlisted, you can refine and improve your pitch for Round 2.

    3. What is the deadline for registration?

      4. The last date to register for Idea Ignite is 28th September 2024. Be sure to submit your application before the deadline to secure your spot.

    1. Is there a participation fee?

      2. No, there is no fee to participate in Idea Ignite. It is a free competition designed to encourage innovation.

    2. What are the evaluation criteria?

      3. Your pitch will be judged on innovation, feasibility, sustainability impact, technicality, and presentation skills. A panel of experts will evaluate each pitch.

    3. Are there any prizes for the winners?

      4. Yes, exciting cash prizes and recognition will be awarded to the top-performing teams, along with All shortlisted candidates will receive mentorship from experienced professionals who will help refine your idea, improve your pitch, and guide you on how to turn it into a viable solution. We’ll assist you in securing Idea Grants and potential funding opportunities through the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Center (IEDC). Our goal is to help you take your idea to the next level. With the right support and guidance, we aim to transform your idea into a product ready for the market. This round isn’t just about presenting your idea—it’s about getting the resources and mentorship you need to bring it to life.

    1. How much time will each team get to pitch?

      2. Each team will have 5-7 minutes to present their idea, followed by a brief Q&A session with the judges.

    2. Can I participate as part of a team?

      3. Yes, participants can form teams ranging from 2 to 4 members, but cannot participate individually.

    3. What technologies or tools can be used in the pitch?

      4. You can use any technology or platform to support your idea, such as prototypes, presentations, or videos. Ensure it aligns with the event's themes of sustainability, blockchain, and AI/ML.

    1. What support will be provided to the participants?

      2. Participants will have access to mentors and resources before the event to refine their ideas and pitches. On the day of the event, technical support will be available for presentations.

    2. What should I bring to the event?

      3. Please bring a laptop or any other device needed to present your idea, along with any supporting materials such as prototypes, presentation slides, or handouts.
